3.Enemy AI System for VR

Description:

The "Enemy AI System for VR" is a cutting-edge development designed for virtual reality applications. It utilizes a Finite State Machine (FSM) state pattern to govern the behavior of enemy AI characters within the VR environment. The AI seamlessly transitions between various states including Idle, Patrol, Chase, Attack, and Investigation, providing players with a dynamic and immersive experience. One of the standout features of this system is its responsiveness to Oculus mic sounds, triggering a swift transition to the investigation state when detecting noise from the player.
